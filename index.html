Course Description

In this course, we will bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We will introduce the fundamental concepts necessary to architect robust applications powered by foundational models and related techniques. Furthermore we would also learn some fundamental software engineering principles that will allow us to begin our journey as a full stack AI engineer.

Topics covered:

  • Advanced RAG Pipelines: Explore how to optimize data retrieval to ensure your models provide accurate, context-aware responses.
  • Agentic Systems: Learn how to design autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and execute multi-step tasks.
  • Model Context Protocol (MCP): Master the techniques for connecting your applications to external data sources and tools to expand model capabilities.
  • Scalable Deployment: Gain hands-on experience with containerization and cloud orchestration to deploy your AI systems in production environments reliably.
